About Secure Social

Secure Social is a cutting-edge platform designed to detect and prevent fake social media accounts. Using machine learning and behavior analytics, we analyze account features like follower ratios, activity patterns, and verified status to identify suspicious or bot-like profiles.

Our mission is to make online communities safer, more transparent, and free from fake engagement.
